RESULTS

17 species found

From the families Passeridae, Estrildidae, Passeridae, Pycnonotidae, Columbidae, Alcedinidae, Apodidae, Accipitridae, Ardeidae, Rallidae, Scolopacidae, Corvidae, and Phasianidae.

Analysis

The results of the analysis with PAST for the Shannon Index are 2,384.

Conclusion

For educational purposes, rice fields can be a laboratory and bird watching practice, while as a tourism area it can become a birdwatching area.

(Linnaeus, 1758)

Java (native), all of Indonesia, East Asia and in many countries
